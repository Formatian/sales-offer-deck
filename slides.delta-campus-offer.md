## What The Delta Campus Material Already Contains

<p class="slide-lead">The source content is not a generic campus story. It is a specific concept stack for Delta Campus: interactive maps, visitor wayfinding, day passes, guided POIs, virtual tours, screens, room booking, event information, operational reporting, and community networking.</p>
