## Navigation And Appendix

<p class="slide-lead">Good appendix design lets you carry dense material without poisoning the main deck pace.</p>

<div class="widget-lab-tabs">
  <button class="widget-lab-tab widget-lab-tab-active" type="button" data-tab-target="commercial">Commercial</button>
  <button class="widget-lab-tab" type="button" data-tab-target="technical">Technical</button>
  <button class="widget-lab-tab" type="button" data-tab-target="operations">Operations</button>
  <button class="widget-lab-tab" type="button" data-tab-target="references">References</button>
</div>

<div class="widget-lab-appendix-grid" data-tab-panel="commercial">
  <div class="widget-lab-appendix-card">
    <span>Expandable clusters</span>
    <strong>Commercial backup: pricing assumptions, service scope, and rollout gates.</strong>
  </div>
  <div class="widget-lab-appendix-card">
    <span>Slide-local navigation</span>
    <strong>Jump directly to price logic, support wrap, or implementation model.</strong>
  </div>
  <div class="widget-lab-appendix-card">
    <span>Modular backup content</span>
    <strong>Keeps the main sales narrative sharp while deeper buying questions stay one click away.</strong>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="widget-lab-appendix-grid" data-tab-panel="technical" hidden>
  <div class="widget-lab-appendix-card">
    <span>System architecture</span>
    <strong>Reader map, middleware layer, event model, and outbound interfaces.</strong>
  </div>
  <div class="widget-lab-appendix-card">
    <span>Hardware footprint</span>
    <strong>Zone coverage, power constraints, mount logic, and serviceability notes.</strong>
  </div>
  <div class="widget-lab-appendix-card">
    <span>Security / IT</span>
    <strong>Network policy, data retention, and handoff to plant IT stakeholders.</strong>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="widget-lab-appendix-grid" data-tab-panel="operations" hidden>
  <div class="widget-lab-appendix-card">
    <span>Shift logic</span>
    <strong>How operators, supervisors, and maintenance teams interact with the flow.</strong>
  </div>
  <div class="widget-lab-appendix-card">
    <span>Exception model</span>
    <strong>Escalation ladders, recovery states, and intervention thresholds.</strong>
  </div>
  <div class="widget-lab-appendix-card">
    <span>Rollout choreography</span>
    <strong>Pilot-to-scale sequence, training needs, and stabilization windows.</strong>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="widget-lab-appendix-grid" data-tab-panel="references" hidden>
  <div class="widget-lab-appendix-card">
    <span>Case proof</span>
    <strong>Short case snapshots, site photos, and measured before / after shifts.</strong>
  </div>
  <div class="widget-lab-appendix-card">
    <span>Client voices</span>
    <strong>Compact quotes tied to operating outcomes rather than generic praise.</strong>
  </div>
  <div class="widget-lab-appendix-card">
    <span>Benchmark pack</span>
    <strong>Use selective benchmarks when internal teams need external comparators.</strong>
  </div>
</div>
