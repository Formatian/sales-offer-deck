## Option 1: QR-Based Virtual Shelf System

<div class="kaldewei-insight-grid">
  <div class="kaldewei-insight-card">
    <span class="metric-label">Operating model</span>
    <strong>Passive tracking with scan events</strong>
    <p>Each load carrier receives a unique QR code. Operators use the mobile FORMATION app to check units in and out of logical storage positions.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="kaldewei-insight-card">
    <span class="metric-label">Captured per scan</span>
    <strong>Identity, timestamp, user, device position</strong>
    <p>Every scan records the asset identity, time, user, and the GPS position of the scanning device at the moment of scan.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="kaldewei-insight-card">
    <span class="metric-label">Why choose it</span>
    <strong>Fastest and lowest-complexity rollout path</strong>
    <p>No fixed tracking infrastructure is required. The setup works indoors and outdoors, supports manual correction, and typically delivers 5 to 10 meter accuracy.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="kaldewei-bottom-band">
  <span class="eyebrow">Indicative annual total</span>
  <p><strong>EUR 36K per year</strong> consisting of passive object tracking for 3,000 assets, 12 user licenses, and the KALDEWEI workspace.</p>
</div>

## Option 2: UWB Tracking with SAP Integration

<div class="kaldewei-commercial-grid">
  <div class="kaldewei-option-card">
    <span class="eyebrow">System logic</span>
    <h3>Same workflow, deeper stack</h3>
    <ul>
      <li>UWB trackers are mounted on handheld scanners, not on every asset.</li>
      <li>Each scan event is enriched with precise real-time scanner position.</li>
      <li>Scan and position data are transferred directly into SAP.</li>
      <li>SAP integration is mandatory for this option to function.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
  <div class="kaldewei-option-card kaldewei-option-card-strong">
    <span class="eyebrow">Operational effect</span>
    <h3>Higher precision for indoor logistics</h3>
    <ul>
      <li>More reliable indoor positioning in dense warehouse environments.</li>
      <li>Less ambiguity when locating carriers and material movements.</li>
      <li>Direct linkage between scan events and SAP material processes.</li>
      <li>Scope is deliberately optimized to avoid unnecessary cost drivers.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="kaldewei-bottom-band">
  <span class="eyebrow">Indicative annual total</span>
  <p><strong>EUR 48K per year</strong> consisting of active tracking, SAP integration and support, 12 user licenses, and the KALDEWEI workspace.</p>
</div>
