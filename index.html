5. Closing

The focus of this proposal is not short-term cost optimization, but establishing a robust, scalable system that can be reliably rolled out and operated over the long term. This is particularly important in production environments where system reliability and operational continuity are critical.

This structure provides a clear and low-risk path to production rollout while maintaining flexibility for future expansion. If we structure the roadmap as a committed delivery block, we can optimize both pricing and delivery efficiency, while accelerating the rollout timeline. We are happy to align on a co-creation approach that balances platform evolution and Lear-specific requirements.

5. Abschluss

Der Fokus dieses Angebots liegt nicht auf kurzfristiger Kostenoptimierung, sondern auf dem Aufbau eines robusten, skalierbaren Systems, das über einen langen Zeitraum zuverlässig ausgerollt und betrieben werden kann.
